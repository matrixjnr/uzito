We are Uzito Afya Foods

Uzito Afya foods ltd subsidiary of The Westport Industrial City Group, is a manufacturer and processor of human and animal foods. The Westport Industrial city is strategically located in Ruiru, with proximity to the Central Business District in Kenya’s capital Nairobi, Kenya.

Uzito Afya Foods sources for superior products, processes and packages its product in eight state of the art warehouses. Our warehouses are equipped with end-to-end warehousing solutions with scientifically designed storage infrastructure. Each warehouse is a Gulf client’s dedicated production, warehousing and packaging plant managed by a unit production manager.
